Uppercase

The uppercase plugin converts lowercase text to uppercase

It operates on bold related tags, viz.:

<b></b> + <em></em> + <strong></strong>

For example

<b title="uppercase">Your Text Here</b>

becomes Your Text Here

It will also operate on any nested tags. eg.:

<strong title="uppercase">Your Text <i>Here</i></strong>

becomes Your Text Here

Just for fun

I have used jQuery to make the text fade out and in at different times.

  • Short text fades the soonest
  • Loooooooooooooooooong text takes the longest
  • Only the length of the first part is counted time-wise if the text is broken up by other tags
